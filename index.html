Welcome to my Web portfolio. On this page you can find examples of professional and personal work that I have chosen to display my skills, interests, and personality. This site is also an example of my work as it is also coded by yours truly.

I have worked professionally as a Technical Writer for a little less than 5 years in a variety of environments. I graduated in 2012 with a B.S. in Technical Communication with a concentration in Information Design from Southern Polytechnic State University (SPSU). As a capstone project for that class, I created a web portfolio that you can view here. However, in today's mobile world, I needed to make sure you could view my portfolio on any device, and so I have discontinued updating my old one and have created this one with modern responsive patterns and bootstrap.

My objective is to apply my technical writing, graphic design, web development, and information design skills as a technical writer in a high energy environment that values professional excellence and providing customers an exceptional experience.
